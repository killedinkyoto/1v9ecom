The Protocol

Simple Ritual.
Compound Results.

No complicated stacks. No 30-pill morning routine. One scoop, mixed in water, taken 20 minutes before the work starts. That's the protocol.

01
Mix One Scoop

Add one scoop (7.5g) to 10–12oz cold water. Stir or shake for 10 seconds. Sour Candy is light and clean — not thick, not sweet, not chemical.

02
Take 20 Min Before

Drink it 15–20 minutes before the session starts. L-Theanine and caffeine peak together. Alpha-GPC onset is gradual — it builds over 30–60 minutes.

03
Lock In

Sit down and work. The stack does its job quietly in the background. No need to think about it. No checking how you feel. Just the work.

Best With
Works on an empty stomach or after a light meal. Avoid stacking with high-dose caffeine. Best results with consistent daily use over 2–4 weeks as compounds accumulate.

You can, but you should be aware of total caffeine intake. Mindor contains 100mg of caffeine per serving. A 12oz cup of coffee contains roughly 100–180mg depending on brew strength. Stacking both puts you at 200–280mg caffeine, which most adults tolerate fine but could cause jitters in caffeine-sensitive individuals. If you do stack, we recommend replacing coffee with Mindor rather than adding on top.

That said, if you're pregnant, nursing, under 18, or on medications — especially blood thinners or stimulants — consult your doctor first.
