What's Inside

7 FAST FIXES.
UNDER 2 MINUTES EACH.

These aren't habits. They're emergency protocols — for the moment you sit down and your brain just won't start.

1
Sensory Reduction
SRD

Remove one input source. Your brain is processing more than you realize — close a tab, kill the music.

2
Tactical Hydration
THD

Dehydration degrades attention before you feel thirsty. A fast, specific fix that most builders skip.

3
Cognitive Offload
COF

Unfinished tasks compete for working memory. Dump every open loop onto paper — free the processor.

4
2-Min Movement Reset
MVR

Sedentary posture reduces blood flow to the brain. Stand, walk 30 steps, reset the cycle.

5
Task Shrink
TSK

Your task is too big. Overwhelm masquerades as brain fog. Cut it until it's small enough to start.

6
Environment Shift
ENV

Change one physical variable. Novel environments reset the default mode network — lighting, seat, location.

7
60-Second Breath Reset
BRT

5 slow breaths: inhale 4 sec, exhale 6 sec. The longer exhale activates your parasympathetic system. Anxiety drops. Clarity returns.
